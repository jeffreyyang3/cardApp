A game very heavily inspired by "Cards Against Humanity".


Instructions:

Each round, one player is randomly selected to be the "card czar".
That player will choose what they think is the best out of 3 question cards.
After the card czar has chosen, the other players each draw ten answers and choose what they think is the best answer.
The card czar then chooses the best answer. Whoever came up with that answer wins the round.
